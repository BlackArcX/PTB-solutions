**Find $\frac{f(a+h)-f(a)}{h}$ and simplify, where** $$f(x)=6x-9$$

$$
\begin{steps}
   f(a+h) &= 6(a+h) - 9
   &= 6a +6h -9
\end{steps}
$$

$$
\begin{steps}
   f(a) &= 6(a) - 9
   &= 6a -9
\end{steps}
$$

$$
\begin{steps}
   \frac{f(a+h)-f(a)}{h} &= \frac{6a +6h -9-(6a -9)}{h}
   &= \frac{6h}{h} = 6
\end{steps}
$$
