**Find $f(x^2+4)$**

1. When $f(x) = x^2 - x$
$$
\begin{split}
   f(x^2+4) &= (x^2+4)^2 - (x^2+4)\\
   &= \left((x^2)^2 + 2(x^2)(4) + 4^2\right) - x^2 - 4\\
   &= x^4 + 8x^2+ 16 - x^2 -4\\
   &= x^4+7x^2+12
\end{split}
$$

2. When $f(x) = \sqrt{x + 4}$
$$
\begin{split}
   f(x^2+4) &= \sqrt{(x^2+4) + 4}\\
   &= \sqrt{x^2+8}
\end{split}
$$
