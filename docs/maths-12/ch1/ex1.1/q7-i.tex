    \textbf{
        Show that the parameteric equations $x=at^2$, $y=2at$
        represent the equation of parabola $y^2=4ax$.
    }

    From equation $y=2at$.

    $$
    t=\frac{y}{2a}
    $$

    Putting in $x=at^2$

    \begin{align*}
        x &= a\left(\frac{y}{2a}\right)^2 \\
            &= a\frac{y^2}{4a^2} \\
        x &= \frac{y^2}{4a} \\
        \\
        \implies y^2 &= 4ax \tag{By Rearranging}
    \end{align*}

    Hence proved.
